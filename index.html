Utilize Your Imagination

Welcome to the Makers Den Alabama. Here, you can learn about the resources and opportunities available to you through our makerspace locations. You can choose between arts and crafts, engineering, online designing, and so much more. Find your hobby, your friends, and your community, today.


Our Makerspaces provide a large range of materials that you can use to help explore your interests and build using your imagination. We believe that with the right tools, you can achieve great things.



What is a Makerspace?

A makerspace can be any space in your school where students and teachers come together to create, invent, prototype, design, tinker, explore, discover, code, build, craft, draft, draw, and more. Students can work individually or collaboratively using a variety of high and low-tech tools and materials.


Why choose our Makerspace?

Our mission is to create a safe atmosphere for innovators and makers like you. We aspire to make a community where creativity is celebrated and imagination is encouraged and nurtured. We aim to provide a place for like-minded individuals to share their passions and learn from each other. We strive to build a culture of learning, exploration, and most importantly building and innovation where everyone has a chance to have as much creativity as possible regardless of background or skill. We do this by supplying all the supplies and materials you could ever need.
