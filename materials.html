Creativity Driven Materials

VEX IQ is a snap-together robotics system designed from the ground up to provide novice users the chance to find success quickly, while still being able to constantly challenge more advanced users.

Kâ€™nex is a very hands-on engineering toy. You can make anything, from a chicken to a model of a rollercoaster that actually works!

Rigamajig is a wooden building kit that allows you to build your own complex systems and explore different forces through engineering and problem-solving.

Kinetic Sand is a type of sand, also known as magic or hydrophobic sand, that holds its shape when squeezed, and can be molded into all different kinds of fun things!

PlayDoh is a modeling compound that allows you to use your imagination and engineer fun designs and sculptures, or use it for a whole bunch of different purposes!

Scratch is a programming language you can use as an introduction to coding and you can create anything from games to animations and stories.
